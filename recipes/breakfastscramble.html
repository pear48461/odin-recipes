Breakfast Scramble

Description

Amazing breakast scramble! Everyone will be so impressed. This recipe is not vegan friendly, and substitutions will affect quality.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 4 Slices Bacon
  • Potatoes, diced
  • Onion, thinly sliced
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a pan over low-medium heat
  2. Add onion and cook until translucent
  3. Add potatoes
  4. Add bacon
  5. Add eggs
  6. Cover and cook over medium heat until everything is done
