Potato Soup

Description

This delicious homemade potato soup is quite the crowd pleaser. It has a rich hearty taste that always makes a great impression. Note that many ingredients can be substituted if needed. Consider replacing the ham with lentils for a vegetarian alternative.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes, peeled and diced
  • Ham, cubed
  • Butter
  • Onion, diced
  • Flour
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Milk or heavy cream
  • Oil

Steps

  1. In a small pan, heat oil
  2. Add onion and cook until translucent
  3. Add garlic and cook 2 more mintes, set aside
  4. Melt butter in a separate pot on the stove
  5. Slowly add flour whlie stirring continuously
  6. Stir for approx. 10 minutes until roux is golden brown
  7. Slowly add in milk continue stirring
  8. Add ham, potatoes, seasonings, and the onion/garlic
  9. Bring pot to a boil, decrease heat, simmer 15 minutes until potatoes are tender
  10. Add milk and simmer 5 more minutes. Add additional seasoning if needed.
