Strawberry Lemonade

Description

Refreshing drink to have on a hot day.

Can add additional flavors like rhubarb or mint.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Lemon
  • Sugar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add strawberries, 1 cup water, and 1/2 cup sugar to a pan
  2. Keep mixture over medium-low heat for 15 minutes - Stir frequently to prevent burning!
  3. Once your strawberries have broken down, you can remove them from the heat and strain the mixture. Strain mixture for a smoother consistency.
  4. Pour mixture into a pitcher and add 6 cups of water.
